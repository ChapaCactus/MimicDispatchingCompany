Game Boy Music + SFX Pack by Feliks Balzer (Pizzamakesgames)

The Game Boy Music + SFX Pack is filled to the brim with music and sound effects, perfectly suited for any game replicating the look and feel of the original Game Boy.

In total, this pack includes:
- 20 Game Boy Loops
- 10 Game Boy Sound Effects

All tracks loop seamlessly and are added to your project by simply dragging the song you want to use into your hierarchy window. Sound effects are suited for single or continuous play.
